Programme structure

Module breakdown

The course is designed to move from core banking knowledge into practical risk and resilience thinking, ending with a final applied exercise that brings business understanding and digital risk judgement together.

Week 1

How investment banking works

  • What investment banks do and how they serve clients.
  • Advisory, markets, trading, research, and support functions.
  • How front office, middle office, and back office interact.
Week 2

Risk and digital exposure

  • Core banking risks and the foundations of risk management.
  • Cyber, data, process, and technology vulnerabilities.
  • Why digital risk matters to performance, confidence, and reputation.
Week 3

Operational resilience and controls

  • Critical services, disruption planning, and control environments.
  • Third-party dependencies and response planning.
  • How resilience supports continuity under stress.
Week 4

Governance and final project

  • Risk governance, escalation, and professional reporting.
  • Decision-making in a regulated financial environment.
  • Final group presentation linking banking activity to digital risk.
